=====Usage notes=====
* ''Vergeben'' implies a greater degree of moral guilt than {{m|de|verzeihen}}. It is used in the context of sin or crime, or otherwise of severe misbehaviour, such as acts of violence, insult, humiliation, deceit, and the like.
* The noun corresponding to the sense “to forgive” is {{m|de|Vergebung}}, while that corresponding to the sense “to assign” is {{m|de|Vergabe}}.
